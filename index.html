Obstacle Course Fun Run!

Overview

Two players race to see who can finish the obstacle course first!

Setup

Player one will be asked to enter a team name. Then, player two will be asked to enter a team name. Once team names are entered, click the green "Run Course" button to begin. Player one will go first.

Gameplay

On your turn, you will be prompted with a description of the obstacle you're on (including the requirements you need to roll in order to proceed to the next obstacle). Click OK on this message to roll the dice. You will then see what number you rolled. Click OK again to read about your fate.

If you successfully run the obstacle, you will move onto the next obstacle right away and continue your turn until you fail.

If you fail the obstacle, you will revisit it on your next turn.

Winning

Win by being the first player to complete all six obstacles! Even if you're not the first, you will still be given an opportunity to finish the whole course.

List of Obstacles

  1. Monkey Bars
  2. Hurdles
  3. Hula Hoop
  4. Tire Course
  5. Rope Climb
  6. Balance Beam
